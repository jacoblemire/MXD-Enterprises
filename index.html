FAQ

Is this “real AI” or scripted?

PlugBot Lite ships as a lightweight, scripted chat experience so you can launch instantly. We also offer optional upgrades that connect to APIs (OpenAI, Formspree, CRMs) when you want more power.

Where do I host the bot?

Anywhere that serves static files: GitHub Pages (free), Netlify, Vercel, or your existing website.

Can I collect form data?

Yes. The templates include an optional hook for Formspree/Google Sheets. Turn it on when you’re ready.

Can you customize the flows for me?

Absolutely. Choose the White‑Label or Done‑for‑You Suite options and we’ll handle everything.
